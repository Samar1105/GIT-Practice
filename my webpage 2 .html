A few facts about the world

Earth, our home, is the third planet from the sun. It's the only planet known to have an atmosphere containing free oxygen, oceans of water on its surface and, of course, life. Earth is the fifth largest of the planets in the solar system. It's smaller than the four gas giants — Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus and Neptune — but larger than the three other rocky planets, Mercury, Mars and Venus.

About the earth

  • Average distance from the sun: 92,956,050 miles (149,598,262 km)
  • Perihelion (closest approach to the sun): 91,402,640 miles (147,098,291 km)
  • Aphelion (farthest distance from the sun): 94,509,460 miles (152,098,233 km)

Top 3 facts about the earth

  1. The Earth’s rotation is gradually slowing.
  2. The Earth was once believed to be the centre of the universe.
  3. Earth has a powerful magnetic field.
